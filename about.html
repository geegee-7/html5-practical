Why I Bake

September 14, 2025

Hello! I'm Georgette, a passionate home baker who loves transforming simple ingredients into delicious desserts.

I created BakeBook to share my favorite recipes with friends, family, and food lovers around the world.

All recipes are written with semantic HTML to ensure accessibility for everyone.

My Go-To Baking Ingredients

  • All-purpose flour
  • Unsalted butter
  • Granulated sugar
  • Vanilla extract
  • Eggs

How I Got Started

  1. Baked my first cake at age 12 (it flopped!)
  2. Tried cookies — success!
  3. Joined a local baking club
  4. Started experimenting with recipes
  5. Launched BakeBook to share my passion

Baking Terms (Definition List)

Creaming
The process of mixing butter and sugar until light and fluffy — a key step in cookie making.
Proofing
Letting dough rise before baking, typically with yeast-based recipes.
Blind Baking
Pre-baking a pie crust without filling, usually by adding weights.
Sifting
Passing dry ingredients through a mesh to remove clumps and aerate.
