Classic Apple Pie

Posted: Sept 10, 2025

  • 6 cups sliced apples
  • 3/4 cup sugar
  • 1 tbsp cinnamon
  • 2 pie crusts (top + bottom)

Bake at 425°F for 45 minutes until golden and bubbly.
